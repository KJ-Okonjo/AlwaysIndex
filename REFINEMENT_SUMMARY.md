## 🎨 Design System Summary

### Colors (Solid Only)
- Primary: #000000 (black), #FFFFFF (white)
- Accent Orange: #fa5f1f
- Accent Navy: #0d1d30
- Accent Blue: #547ba4
- Accent Gray: #666A86
- Accent Teal: #558C8C
- Accent Red: #C62025

### Typography
- Font: Inter (sans-serif)
- Weights: 400, 500, 600, 700, 800
- Clean, professional hierarchy

### Layout
- No border-radius (sharp corners)
- 1px borders (rgba(0,0,0,0.1))
- Clean geometric grids
- Generous white space

### Buttons
- Standard size: 1rem padding (1rem × 2rem)
- Contact CTAs: 1.25rem padding (1.25rem × 2.5rem)
- No rounded corners
- Solid color fills
- Minimal hover effects (translateY -2px)
