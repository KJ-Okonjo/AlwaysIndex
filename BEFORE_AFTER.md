## Content Changes

### Pricing Features - What Was Cut

#### Starter (Removed):
- Basic SEO setup
- 1 round of revisions
- 14 days post-launch support

#### Professional (Removed):
- Newsletter integration
- Social media integration
- 2 rounds of revisions
- 30 days post-launch support

#### Premium (Removed):
- Inventory management system
- Custom admin dashboard
- 3 rounds of revisions
- 60 days post-launch support

### Why These Were Removed
Focus on **core deliverables** only. Support details and revision counts can be discussed during consultation rather than cluttering the pricing display.

## Design Philosophy

### Before (V1)
- Bold, flashy, attention-grabbing
- Heavy use of gradients and effects
- More "startup" aesthetic
- Extensive feature lists

### After (V2)
- Clean, professional, trust-building
- Solid colors, clear hierarchy
- More "established agency" aesthetic
- Focused, curated information

## Inspiration Sources

### V1 Inspiration
- Modern startup landing pages
- Gradient-heavy design trends
- Animated, dynamic experiences

### V2 Inspiration
- fff.club - Professional investment platform
- digiface.agency - Clean service presentation
- are.na curated websites - Refined, editorial style

---

**Summary**: V2 represents a maturation of the brand from "exciting startup" to "trusted professional service." The design prioritizes clarity, professionalism, and conversion over visual spectacle.
