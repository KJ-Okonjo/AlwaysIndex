FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to launch my site?

+

Our standard timeline is 7 days from project kickoff to launch. For our Starter package, delivery is typically 7-10 days. Professional packages are delivered within 7 days, and Premium packages in 5-7 days. Need it faster? Our Express upgrade delivers in just 72 hours.

Do you only work with clothing brands?

+

While we specialize in clothing brands and fashion businesses, we also work with creators, influencers, startups, and various other businesses. If you need a premium website delivered quickly, we can help.

Can you redesign my existing website?

+

Yes! We can redesign your existing website to give it a modern, fresh look. Contact us to discuss your current site and what improvements you're looking for.

Do you offer ongoing support?

+

All packages include post-launch support (14-60 days depending on your package). For ongoing maintenance, technical support, and updates, we offer a monthly maintenance plan at ₦50,000/month.

What if I need changes after launch?

+

Each package includes revision rounds (1-3 depending on tier) before launch. After launch, you have support coverage for technical issues. Additional changes can be handled through our maintenance plan or as separate projects.

Do I need to provide content and images?

+

Yes, you'll need to provide your brand assets, content, and images. We can offer guidance on content structure and image requirements. If you need professional copywriting or photography, we can recommend trusted partners.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept bank transfers and can provide payment plans for Premium packages. A 50% deposit is required to start your project, with the balance due before launch.
